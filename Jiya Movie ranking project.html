Movies Ranking List by Jiya

Top 3 movies

Your Lie in April

"Your Lie in April" is a popular anime movie that tells the poignant story of a young pianist named Kousei Arima, who loses his ability to hear the sound of the piano after his mother's death. He leads a colorless life until he meets an eccentric and free-spirited violinist, Kaori Miyazono, who helps him rediscover his love for music and life. The movie beautifully explores themes of love, friendship, and the healing power of art as Kousei and Kaori navigate the challenges of their respective pasts and pursue their dreams in the world of classical music. It's a touching and emotional journey that resonates with viewers through its music and characters.

Me Before You

"Me Before You" tells the story of a young woman named Louisa "Lou" Clark, who becomes the caregiver for a wealthy quadriplegic man named Will Traynor. As Lou and Will spend time together, a deep and transformative relationship develops between them. The movie explores themes of love, life, and personal choice, as well as the challenges of living with a disability. It's a heartwarming and emotional tale of two individuals from different worlds who find solace and inspiration in each other's company.

Five Feet Apart

"Five Feet Apart" is an emotionally gripping and heart-wrenching love story that captivates the audience with its poignant portrayal of two teenagers, Stella and Will, who are both battling cystic fibrosis. Their forbidden romance unfolds beautifully against the backdrop of strict medical protocols, which mandate they must maintain a five-foot distance to avoid cross-infection.This film beautifully navigates themes of love, resilience, and the indomitable human spirit, making it a must-watch for anyone seeking a compelling and emotional cinematic experience.
